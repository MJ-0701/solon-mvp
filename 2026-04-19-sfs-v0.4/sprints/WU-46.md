# WU-46 — Non-Dev Division Policy Ladders

## §0. Intent

Balance the WU-35/WU-45 backend-heavy guardrails by giving the other Solon
divisions concrete first-pass operating policies.

## §1. Policies Added

- Strategy-PM: lean PRD and AC first; metric/segment/pricing/support/roadmap
  review only when positioning, monetization, launch, retention, or stakeholder
  promises are affected; pivot/roadmap/pricing changes require user acceptance.
- Taxonomy: lightweight glossary first; canonical model, alias policy, and
  schema versioning when terms become cross-division or persisted; rename/schema
  migration requires approval.
- Design/frontend: existing design system and basic UX/accessibility first;
  tokens/component/interaction contracts when screens and repeated components
  grow; redesign/navigation/system replacement requires acceptance.
- QA: AC smoke first; risk matrix, integration/E2E, and flaky-test policy when
  risk grows; Release Readiness gate only when production exposure or rollback
  risk appears.
- Infra: local/single deploy hygiene first; CI/CD/secrets/monitoring/runbook
  when deploying beyond local; SLO/multi-region/k8s/IAM/compliance requires
  explicit approval.
